What is a Non-Owner Policy?

A non-owner auto policy provides liability coverage when you drive a car you don't own — rental cars, borrowed vehicles, car shares. It's the #1 way to satisfy an SR-22 requirement without owning a vehicle.

  • 📋
    Fulfill your SR-22 requirement — Get your license reinstated even without owning a car
  • 💰
    Super affordable — Typically 30-50% less than a standard auto policy
  • 🚗
    Drive any car — Covered when borrowing, renting, or using rideshare
  • 📈
    Build continuous coverage — Start building a clean insurance history now to lower future rates
  • ✅
    Stay legal in NC — Maintain compliance and avoid additional fines or penalties

The SR-22 filing itself is usually just $15-25. Your insurance rate will be higher due to the underlying violation, but we shop multiple carriers to minimize that cost. Many of our SR-22 clients get policies starting around $80-150/month depending on their record. Non-owner SR-22 policies are even cheaper.
If budget is the priority, we can set you up with minimum liability coverage (NC's required 30/60/25 limits) with the lowest down payment available. Some carriers offer down payments starting at $49 with affordable monthly payments. If you don't own a car, a non-owner policy is even cheaper. Call us and we'll find the most affordable option for your situation.
NC takes lapses seriously — your insurance company notifies the DMV automatically. You could face fines of $50-$150, plate revocation, and your rates go up. The good news? The sooner you get re-insured, the less damage. We can get you covered fast and help minimize the penalties. Don't wait.
Drivers under 25 are statistically in more accidents, so every carrier charges more. But the difference between carriers can be hundreds of dollars. That's where we come in — we compare all 6 of our carriers to find the one that treats your age group the fairest. Plus, NC now has an 8-year inexperienced driver surcharge (up from 3 years), so getting the right carrier matters more than ever.
A non-owner policy covers you when driving cars you don't own — rentals, borrowed vehicles, etc. You need one if: you have an SR-22 requirement but no car, you regularly borrow or rent vehicles, or you want to build continuous coverage history to lower your future rates. They're typically 30-50% cheaper than standard auto policies.
